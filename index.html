About the BCA Department

TechVerse represents innovation, technology, and creativity. The BCA program at Baba Farid College, Bathinda focuses on real-world problem-solving with modern tools and industry-level learning.


Students get hands-on experience in programming, app development, networking, AI tools, data structures, and more.
